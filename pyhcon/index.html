PyCon Nigeria Annual Conference.


The PyConNG 2017 conference, which will take place in Lagos,is the largest annual gathering for the python community in Nigeria using the open-source Python programming language.

ABOUT PYCON

It is Organised by members of the Python Users Nigeria Group, a nonprofit organisation dedicated to advancing and promoting the use of Python programming language in the country. Through PyCon, The Python Users Nigeria Group advances its mission of growing the community of Python programmers in Nigeria. This is the first year of PyCon Nigeria where the community will meet for two days of talks and collaboration on projects in two days of learning, collaboration, and development sprint.
